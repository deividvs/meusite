Do I need land to start?

+

No! We cover multiple approaches including leasing agreements, cooperative grazing, and small-scale operations that don't require large acreage.

What if I don't own land?

+

Many of our successful students started with rented pasture or small acreage. We dedicate an entire section to making arrangements with landowners and maximizing small spaces. You might be surprised how little space you actually need to start.

What kind of cattle is best for beginners?

+

We recommend starting with beef cattle breeds that are known for their hardiness and good temperament. The course includes detailed comparisons of different breeds and what to look for when buying your first animals.
